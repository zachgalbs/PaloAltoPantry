About Us

Our Mission

Our organization is dedicated to combating hunger by providing essential food support to those in need. We focus on collecting and distributing nutritious food to underprivileged individuals and families, ensuring no one in our community suffers from food scarcity. Through collaborations with local entities and volunteers, we address immediate needs while building a foundation for lasting food resilience, all guided by the belief that access to food is a fundamental human right.

Our mission is to alleviate hunger by providing essential food aid to underprivileged communities, ensuring no one faces the hardship of food scarcity. Through partnerships and a commitment to sustainable solutions, we uphold the belief that access to food is a fundamental human right.

Our Partnership

We are excited to collaborate with the LifeMoves organization in order to deliver nourishing hot meals on Monday evenings (prepared in the kitchen of Saint Michael’s Alley) as well as needed canned and processed foods to local residents with food and housing insecurities. Stay tuned as we will update this site with more details & images soon.

With the help of the LifeMoves organization, we are extending our reach to a Mountain View shelter, delivering hot meals to those in need. All canned and processed foods that we receive will be distributed throughout Palo Alto
